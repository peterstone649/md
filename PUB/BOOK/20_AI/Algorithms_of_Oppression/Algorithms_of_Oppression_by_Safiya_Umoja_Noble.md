## Overview

**Algorithms of Oppression: How Search Engines Reinforce Racism** is a powerful and incisive work by Safiya Umoja Noble, published in 2018, that exposes how search engines, particularly Google, systematically perpetuate racism and sexism through their algorithmic design and business practices. Noble, an information scientist and professor, combines rigorous research with compelling analysis to reveal how the supposedly neutral algorithms that power our information access are deeply embedded with bias and discrimination.
