## Overview

**Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (AIMA)**, first published in 1995 and now in its 4th edition (2020), is the definitive textbook on artificial intelligence. Co-authored by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig, this comprehensive work covers the breadth of AI from foundational concepts to cutting-edge research. The book presents AI as a scientific discipline focused on creating machines capable of intelligent behavior, while emphasizing the importance of rationality, learning, and adaptation.
