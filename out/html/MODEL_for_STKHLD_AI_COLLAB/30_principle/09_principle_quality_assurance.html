9. Principle of Quality Assurance (PRIN_MSHCOL_QUALITY_ASSURANCE) [PRIO: HIGH]

Version: V0.1.0 Status: OPEN Date: 2026-01-09

We establish quality assurance as a core principle requiring systematic validation of stakeholder-AI outputs against defined standards.

Objectives: 1. Standard Definition: Establish clear quality criteria 2. Validation Processes: Implement systematic review methods 3. Defect Detection: Identify and correct quality issues 4. Continuous Improvement: Enhance quality over time

Abstract

[AI_LOCK] The Principle of Quality Assurance establishes that stakeholder-AI collaboration must include systematic validation of outputs against defined quality standards. This principle ensures that AI-assisted work meets stakeholder requirements, maintains consistency, and achieves desired outcomes. Quality assurance combines human judgment with systematic processes to verify and maintain output quality. [END_AI_LOCK]
