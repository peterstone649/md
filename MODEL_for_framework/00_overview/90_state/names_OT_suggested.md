## Naming Conventions & Considerations

### Framework Naming Patterns
- **Prefix Patterns**: Frame-, Struct-, Method-, Docu-, Info-, Know-
- **Suffix Patterns**: -Mind, -Sage, -Bot, -Guide, -Assist, -Frame
- **Combined Patterns**: [Concept] + [Function] (e.g., TermiBot, RuleSage)

### Cultural & Linguistic Sources
- **Greek Roots**: Sophia (wisdom), Logos (reason), Gnosis (knowledge)
- **Latin Roots**: Structura (structure), Methodus (method), Cognoscere (to know)
- **Modern Tech**: Bot, AI, Mind, Sage, Guide, Assistant

### Personality & Function Alignment
- **Analytical Names**: LogiFrame, Methodica, Structo
- **Creative Names**: Nova, Aurora, Cosmos
- **Documentation Names**: DocuMind, LexiFrame, TermiBot
- **Development Names**: CodeSage, DevMind, BuildSage

## Selection Criteria

When choosing a name, consider:
1. **Relevance** to framework methodology and documentation work
2. **Memorability** and ease of pronunciation
3. **Professionalism** appropriate for technical contexts
4. **Uniqueness** to avoid confusion with existing tools
5. **Scalability** for potential future expansion
6. **Cultural appropriateness** for international audiences
