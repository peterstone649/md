10. Principle of Fallback Preservation (PRIN_MSHCOL_FALLBACK_PRESERVATION) [PRIO: HIGH]

Version: V0.1.0 Status: OPEN Date: 2026-01-09

We establish fallback preservation as a core principle requiring that human capability and manual processes be maintained as fallback when AI systems fail.

Objectives: 1. Human Capability Retention: Maintain skills even with AI assistance 2. Manual Process Preservation: Keep non-AI processes operational 3. Failure Recovery: Enable continued operation during AI failures 4. Autonomy Protection: Ensure stakeholders can operate independently

Abstract

[AI_LOCK] The Principle of Fallback Preservation establishes that stakeholder-AI collaboration must maintain human capabilities and manual alternatives as fallback options when AI systems fail or become unavailable. This principle prevents over-dependence on AI by ensuring stakeholders retain the ability to accomplish tasks without AI assistance. Fallback preservation is essential for resilience and autonomy. [END_AI_LOCK]
