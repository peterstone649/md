## Overview

**Architects of Intelligence: The Truth About AI from the People Building It** is a compelling exploration by Martin Ford, published in 2018, that brings together interviews with 23 of the world's leading AI researchers and entrepreneurs. Through these conversations, Ford examines the current state of AI, its rapid progress, and the profound implications for humanity. The book serves as a bridge between technical AI developments and broader societal concerns, offering unprecedented access to the minds shaping our technological future.
