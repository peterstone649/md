## Overview

**Race After Technology: Abolitionist Tools for the New Jim Code** is a powerful and incisive work by Ruha Benjamin, published in 2019, that exposes how technology is not neutral but actively reinforces racial hierarchies and perpetuates systemic racism. Benjamin, a sociologist and associate professor at Princeton University, introduces the concept of the "New Jim Code" to describe how coded bias in technology creates new forms of racial discrimination that are often invisible and automated.
