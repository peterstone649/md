Stakeholder-AI Collaboration Framework (NEW)

The framework establishes 12 foundational principles for effective human-AI collaboration: 1. Human Sovereignty: Humans retain final decision-making authority 2. Transparency: AI involvement is fully disclosed and traceable 3. Proportionality: AI assistance levels match task complexity and risk 4. Accountability: Clear responsibility chains for all collaborative outputs 5. Iterative Validation: Systematic human review at critical checkpoints 6. Context Preservation: Critical information remains intact throughout collaboration 7. Reversibility: All AI-assisted changes are reversible and auditable 8. Incremental Integration: Gradual AI capability introduction with monitoring 9. Quality Assurance: Systematic validation against defined standards 10. Fallback Preservation: Human capability maintained as fallback when AI fails 11. Bias Mitigation: Active detection and correction of AI-generated biases 12. Continuous Learning: Framework evolves based on stakeholder feedback
