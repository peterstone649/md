## üìã Quick Feedback Guidelines

1. **Be specific** - The more details, the better we can address your feedback
2. **Be constructive** - Focus on what would make the framework better
3. **Be kind** - We appreciate all feedback, positive or negative
4. **One topic per submission** - Keep each feedback focused on a single issue/idea
5. **Use clear language** - Write as you would explain to a colleague
