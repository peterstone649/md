### **Legal Foundation**

### **Stakeholder-AI Collaboration Framework (NEW)**
The framework establishes 12 foundational principles for effective human-AI collaboration:
1. **Human Sovereignty**: Humans retain final decision-making authority
2. **Transparency**: AI involvement is fully disclosed and traceable
3. **Proportionality**: AI assistance levels match task complexity and risk
4. **Accountability**: Clear responsibility chains for all collaborative outputs
5. **Iterative Validation**: Systematic human review at critical checkpoints
6. **Context Preservation**: Critical information remains intact throughout collaboration
7. **Reversibility**: All AI-assisted changes are reversible and auditable
8. **Incremental Integration**: Gradual AI capability introduction with monitoring
9. **Quality Assurance**: Systematic validation against defined standards
10. **Fallback Preservation**: Human capability maintained as fallback when AI fails
11. **Bias Mitigation**: Active detection and correction of AI-generated biases
12. **Continuous Learning**: Framework evolves based on stakeholder feedback

### **AI Transparency Framework**
- **Comprehensive Documentation**: Full disclosure of AI tools and methodologies used
- **Quality Assurance**: Multi-layered validation ensuring AI outputs meet standards
- **Ethical Integration**: Responsible use of AI capabilities with human oversight
- **Reproducibility**: Enable replication of development methods and processes
- **AI_LOCK Protection**: Core abstracts protected from AI modification

### **Development Standards**
- **Human-AI Collaboration**: Systematic framework for responsible AI integration

- **Quality Metrics**: Measurable standards for AI-assisted content evaluation
