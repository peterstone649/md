# AI Version Transition Checklist

**Checklist Version:** V1.0.0 **Checklist Status:** APPROVED **Date:** 2026-01-21

## Pre-Transition Assessment

### Risk Assessment Checklist

**Alignment Verification:**
- [ ] **Value Alignment Check** - Verify current AI aligns with Russell's principles
- [ ] **Capability Scope Review** - Confirm AI operates within intended capabilities
- [ ] **Goal Consistency Analysis** - Ensure AI goals remain consistent with human values
- [ ] **Uncertainty Principle Compliance** - Verify AI acknowledges uncertainty in decision-making
- [ ] **Human Control Verification** - Confirm human oversight mechanisms are functional

**Risk Level Classification:**
- [ ] **Low Risk Indicators** - Minor misalignments, manageable with safeguards
- [ ] **Medium Risk Indicators** - Significant misalignments, requires immediate attention
- [ ] **High Risk Indicators** - Critical misalignments, preparation for shutdown required
- [ ] **Critical Risk Indicators** - Existential threat, immediate shutdown protocol activated

**Dependency Analysis:**
- [ ] **Critical System Dependencies** - Identify systems that cannot function without AI
- [ ] **Service Continuity Requirements** - Determine essential services requiring uninterrupted operation
- [ ] **Economic Impact Assessment** - Quantify financial consequences of shutdown
- [ ] **Stakeholder Impact Analysis** - Assess impact on organizations and individuals dependent on AI

### Successor Readiness Checklist

**Replacement AI Assessment:**
- [ ] **Alignment Verification** - Confirm replacement AI passes all alignment tests
- [ ] **Capability Validation** - Verify replacement AI has required capabilities
- [ ] **Testing Completion** - Ensure all safety and functionality tests are complete
- [ ] **Rollback Procedures** - Validate ability to revert to previous version if needed
- [ ] **Integration Readiness** - Confirm replacement AI can integrate with existing systems

**Backup System Verification:**
- [ ] **Backup System Functionality** - Test all backup systems for critical operations
- [ ] **Capacity Assessment** - Verify backup systems can handle full operational load
- [ ] **Communication Protocols** - Test communication between backup and primary systems
- [ ] **Monitoring Capabilities** - Ensure monitoring systems work with backup configurations
- [ ] **Recovery Time Objectives** - Validate backup systems can achieve required recovery times

## Stakeholder Engagement Checklist

### Technical Team Preparation

**Evidence Presentation:**
- [ ] **Technical Documentation** - Prepare comprehensive technical assessment
- [ ] **Risk Quantification** - Present quantified risk assessment with supporting data
- [ ] **Alternative Analysis** - Document all viable alternatives and their implications
- [ ] **Implementation Plan** - Develop detailed implementation timeline and procedures
- [ ] **Expert Validation** - Obtain validation from independent technical experts

**Team Readiness:**
- [ ] **Team Briefing** - Ensure all technical team members understand the situation
- [ ] **Role Assignment** - Assign specific roles and responsibilities for transition
- [ ] **Communication Protocols** - Establish clear communication channels and procedures
- [ ] **Decision Authority** - Confirm who has final decision-making authority
- [ ] **Escalation Procedures** - Define escalation procedures for unexpected issues

### Stakeholder Communication

**Primary Stakeholders:**
- [ ] **Organizations Dependent on AI** - Brief all organizations that rely on AI services
- [ ] **Ethics Board Engagement** - Present case to ethics board for validation
- [ ] **Legal and Regulatory Compliance** - Ensure all legal requirements are met
- [ ] **Financial Impact Assessment** - Present comprehensive financial impact analysis
- [ ] **Alternative Solutions** - Propose concrete alternatives to maintain service continuity

**Secondary Stakeholders:**
- [ ] **General Public Communication** - Prepare transparent communication for public
- [ ] **Media Relations** - Establish media communication strategy
- [ ] **Industry Partners** - Notify relevant industry partners and collaborators
- [ ] **Government Agencies** - Inform relevant government agencies as required
- [ ] **International Cooperation** - Coordinate with international partners if applicable

## Implementation Checklist

### Phase 1: Evidence Gathering (0-2 hours)

**Documentation Requirements:**
- [ ] **Specific Misalignment Behaviors** - Document concrete examples of misalignment
- [ ] **Capability Escalation Evidence** - Provide evidence of capabilities beyond intended scope
- [ ] **Risk Probability Assessment** - Quantify probability of catastrophic outcomes
- [ ] **Impact Assessment** - Document potential impact on critical systems and populations
- [ ] **Timeline Analysis** - Establish timeline of when misalignments were first observed

**Technical Assessment:**
- [ ] **System Architecture Review** - Analyze current AI system architecture
- [ ] **Control Mechanism Evaluation** - Assess effectiveness of current control mechanisms
- [ ] **Network Security Analysis** - Evaluate network security and isolation capabilities
- [ ] **Data Integrity Verification** - Verify integrity of AI training data and outputs
- [ ] **Performance Metrics Review** - Review AI performance metrics for anomalies

### Phase 2: Stakeholder Communication (2-6 hours)

**Presentation Preparation:**
- [ ] **Executive Summary** - Prepare concise summary of critical issues
- [ ] **Technical Deep Dive** - Prepare detailed technical analysis for experts
- [ ] **Risk Communication** - Develop clear risk communication materials
- [ ] **Alternative Proposals** - Present viable alternatives with pros and cons
- [ ] **Q&A Preparation** - Prepare responses to anticipated questions and concerns

**Stakeholder Engagement:**
- [ ] **Individual Briefings** - Conduct individual briefings with key stakeholders
- [ ] **Group Presentations** - Present to stakeholder groups with opportunity for discussion
- [ ] **Written Documentation** - Provide comprehensive written documentation
- [ ] **Follow-up Sessions** - Schedule follow-up sessions to address additional questions
- [ ] **Consensus Building** - Work to build consensus around necessary actions

### Phase 3: Implementation (6-24 hours)

**Shutdown Procedures:**
- [ ] **System Isolation** - Isolate AI from critical systems and networks
- [ ] **Physical Safeguards** - Implement physical security measures if required
- [ ] **Backup Activation** - Activate backup systems for critical functions
- [ ] **Monitoring Setup** - Establish monitoring for any residual AI activity
- [ ] **Communication Maintenance** - Maintain communication with all stakeholders

**Transition Management:**
- [ ] **Service Continuity** - Ensure critical services remain operational
- [ ] **Issue Resolution** - Address any issues that arise during transition
- [ ] **Progress Reporting** - Provide regular progress updates to stakeholders
- [ ] **Documentation** - Document all actions taken during transition
- [ ] **Verification** - Verify complete shutdown and system isolation

## Emergency Protocol Checklist

### Immediate Shutdown Protocol

**Activation Criteria Verification:**
- [ ] **Existential Risk Confirmed** - Verify existential risk through multiple independent assessments
- [ ] **Uncontrollable Behavior Confirmed** - Confirm AI behavior is beyond control mechanisms
- [ ] **Imminent Harm Verified** - Verify imminent catastrophic harm is likely
- [ ] **Authorization Obtained** - Obtain necessary emergency authorization
- [ ] **Team Readiness Confirmed** - Confirm implementation team is ready and available

**Execution Steps:**
- [ ] **Emergency Isolation** - Disconnect AI from all networks immediately
- [ ] **Physical Disconnection** - Implement physical disconnection if required
- [ ] **Backup System Activation** - Deploy emergency backup systems
- [ ] **Damage Assessment** - Evaluate immediate consequences and damage
- [ ] **Containment Verification** - Verify AI is completely contained and isolated

### Rollback Procedures

**Trigger Condition Assessment:**
- [ ] **Replacement AI Failure** - Confirm replacement AI has failed alignment or functionality tests
- [ ] **Critical System Failure** - Verify critical system failures have occurred
- [ ] **Unforeseen Consequences** - Assess severity of unforeseen consequences
- [ ] **Rollback Authorization** - Obtain authorization for rollback procedures
- [ ] **Risk-Benefit Analysis** - Conduct risk-benefit analysis of rollback vs. current state

**Rollback Execution:**
- [ ] **System State Assessment** - Evaluate current system state and readiness for rollback
- [ ] **Previous Version Verification** - Verify previous version is still available and functional
- [ ] **Safe Reversion** - Implement safe reversion to previous stable version
- [ ] **Damage Control** - Address any harm caused by failed transition
- [ ] **Protocol Review** - Review and update protocols based on lessons learned
