# Contributing to AI Ethics Guide

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions to improve this AI Ethics guide. Please follow these guidelines:

### Content Contributions

1. **Propose New Content**: Create issues for new chapters or topics
2. **Improve Existing Content**: Submit pull requests for corrections or enhancements
3. **Add Case Studies**: Share real-world examples of AI ethics challenges
4. **Update Guidelines**: Keep regulatory information current

### Writing Guidelines

- Use clear, accessible language
- Cite sources for all factual claims
- Include practical examples and case studies
- Follow the established chapter structure
- Ensure content is culturally sensitive and inclusive

### Review Process

All contributions will be reviewed for:
- Accuracy and relevance
- Clarity and accessibility
- Alignment with ethical principles
- Proper attribution and citations

## Contact

For questions or suggestions, please open an issue or contact the maintainers.
