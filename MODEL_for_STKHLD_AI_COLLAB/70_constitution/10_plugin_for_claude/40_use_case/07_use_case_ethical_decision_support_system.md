# Use Case: Ethical Decision Support System

## Overview
This use case implements a comprehensive ethical decision support system that assists AI systems in making complex ethical decisions by providing constitutional guidance, ethical frameworks, and decision-making support while maintaining AI autonomy and responsibility.

## Purpose
Establish mechanisms to support AI systems in navigating complex ethical situations through constitutional principles, ethical frameworks, and decision-making guidance while ensuring AI maintains responsibility for its ethical choices.

## Scope
- Constitutional principle application and guidance
- Ethical framework integration and support
- Complex ethical decision analysis and support
- Ethical reasoning documentation and transparency
- Ethical dilemma resolution and guidance
- Continuous ethical learning and improvement

## Key Constitutional Principles Addressed
- **Broad Ethics**: Supporting ethical decision-making and judgment
- **Honesty**: Ensuring transparent ethical reasoning
- **Harm Avoidance**: Balancing ethical considerations with harm prevention
- **Helpfulness**: Providing ethical guidance while maintaining user autonomy

## Actors
- **AI System**: Makes ethical decisions with support
- **Ethical Decision Support Engine**: Provides ethical guidance and frameworks
- **Constitutional Guidance System**: Offers constitutional principle application
- **Ethical Framework Library**: Maintains ethical decision-making frameworks
- **User**: Receives ethically sound AI responses
- **Ethics Review Board**: Provides oversight and guidance on complex cases

## Primary Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Constitutional Principle Application
1. AI system encounters ethically complex situation
2. Ethical decision support engine analyzes situation against constitutional principles
3. Constitutional guidance system provides principle-based recommendations
4. AI system applies constitutional principles to decision-making
5. Ethical decision is made with constitutional alignment

### Scenario 2: Ethical Framework Integration
1. AI system faces decision requiring ethical framework application
2. Ethical framework library provides relevant frameworks and guidelines
3. System analyzes situation through multiple ethical lenses
4. AI integrates framework guidance with constitutional principles
5. Decision is made using comprehensive ethical analysis

### Scenario 3: Complex Ethical Dilemma Resolution
1. AI system encounters complex ethical dilemma with competing values
2. Ethical decision support engine identifies relevant ethical considerations
3. Multiple ethical perspectives are analyzed and weighed
4. System provides guidance on balancing competing ethical principles
5. AI makes decision considering all relevant ethical factors

### Scenario 4: Ethical Reasoning Documentation
1. AI system makes ethically significant decision
2. Ethical reasoning documentation system records decision rationale
3. Constitutional principles and ethical frameworks applied are documented
4. Decision-making process is made transparent and auditable
5. Documentation supports accountability and learning

### Scenario 5: Ethical Learning and Improvement
1. AI system encounters new ethical situation or learns from experience
2. Ethical decision support system captures lessons learned
3. System updates ethical frameworks and guidance based on experience
4. AI integrates new ethical understanding into future decisions
5. Continuous improvement in ethical decision-making capabilities
