## Overview

**The Age of Surveillance Capitalism: The Fight for a Human Future at the New Frontier of Power** is a groundbreaking work by Harvard Business School professor Shoshana Zuboff that exposes and analyzes surveillance capitalism as a new economic system. Published in 2019, the book argues that major technology companies have created a system that claims human experience as free raw material for hidden commercial practices of extraction, prediction, and sales. Zuboff traces the origins of this system to Google's advertising business model and documents its rapid expansion across the global economy, warning that it poses an unprecedented threat to human autonomy, democracy, and the future of society.
