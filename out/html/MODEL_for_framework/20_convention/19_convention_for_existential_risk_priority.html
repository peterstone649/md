MAXIMUM Priority Definition

Criteria for MAXIMUM Classification

An issue is classified as MAXIMUM when it meets ANY of the following:

  1. Civilization Collapse Potential
  2. Risk of human extinction
  3. Risk of irreversible global catastrophe

  4. Risk of permanent reduction in human potential (>50% population)

  5. Uncontrolled AI Scenarios

  6. Loss of human control over AI systems
  7. AI optimization of harmful objectives

  8. Recursive self-improvement without alignment

  9. Irreversibility

  10. Actions cannot be undone
  11. No recovery path exists

  12. Point of no return crossed or imminent

  13. Speed of Onset

  14. Less than 24 hours from detection to impact
  15. Insufficient time for coordinated response
  16. Exponential growth dynamics
