**Multi-Species Coherent Extrapolated Volition (MS-CEV):**
- **Definition**: What multi-species would want if they knew more, thought faster, were more the people they wished they were, and had grown up closer together
- **Scope**: All sentient species and critical ecosystem components
- **Timeline**: Continuous evolution over 100-year recovery period

**Evolutionary Mechanisms:**
- **Natural Selection Integration**: Incorporating evolutionary biology principles

- **Mandatory Review**: All multi-species values reviewed every 10 years
- **Sunset Clauses**: Automatic expiration without renewal and re-evaluation
- **Minority Protection**: Protection of minority species and ecosystem interests
- **Adaptive Flexibility**: Framework designed to adapt to new information and circumstances
